How to Update Your Vivomove 3

You can update the Garmin Vivomove 3 using either the Garmin Connect app or Garmin Express software:

  • Garmin Connect: Open the app, go to your device settings, and check for available updates. If an update is available, follow the prompts to install it.
  • Garmin Express: Connect your watch to a computer using a USB cable. Open Garmin Express, and it will prompt you if an update is available.

Ensure your device is charged and connected to the internet before starting the update process.
